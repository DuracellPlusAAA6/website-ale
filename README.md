# vatamanelu.com

Personal website for **Dr. Alexandra Vatamanelu** — Medical Intern in Neurology at [CHU de Nîmes](https://www.chu-nimes.fr), France.

Live at: **https://vatamanelu.com**

## What it is

A single-page CV/profile site for a neurologist. It includes:

- Hero section with name, title, and photo
- **Why Neurology** — a personal statement on her specialty choice
- **Professional experience** — timeline of internships in Romania and France
- **Education** — MD from Université de Médecine et Pharmacie Grigore T. Popa, Iași
- **Skills** and **Languages** (Romanian, French, English, Spanish, Portuguese)
- **Contact** — email revealed after Google reCAPTCHA v3 verification to reduce bot scraping
- Language switcher: Français / English / Română / Español / Português
